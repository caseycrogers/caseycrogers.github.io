Mesh Maker

Mesh Maker is a ~800 line Python add-in I wrote for Autodesk Fusion360. It takes in triangulated 3D objects as .stl files and converts them into a series of small interlocking and 3D printable triangles exported as a series of .stl files. Binary bit patterns embedded into the triangles guides the user through assembly.

Mesh Maker takes large complex objects with structures challenging to 3D printing and makes them easily printable on a consumer grade FDM machine. The result is both artistic and and funtional for rapid prototyping.
